Student Services

These are the services available to support your academic and personal growth.

Tutoring Services

We offer one-on-one and group tutoring sessions to help you excel in your studies. Whether you need help with math, science, or any other subject, we are here for you.

Career Counseling

Our career services can guide you in finding internships, preparing resumes, and job searching. We aim to help you succeed in your career journey.

IT Support

If you're experiencing technical issues with your devices or software, our IT support team is available to help you troubleshoot and resolve problems efficiently.

Mental Health Support

We offer counseling and resources to support your mental well-being. Whether you're feeling stressed, anxious, or need someone to talk to, we're here for you.

Library Access

The library provides a wealth of academic resources, including books, online journals, and study spaces. It's a great place to study and gather materials for your coursework.
